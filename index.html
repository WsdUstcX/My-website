Shouda Wang 王首达

Starting this September, I will be a postdoc at KTH, working with Petter Brändén. My research interests lie in convex geometry, geometric inequalities, and their interactions with matroid theory.

Previously, I obtained my Ph.D. from Princeton University, where I was advised by Ramon van Handel. Before that, I was a student at École Polytechnique; earlier, I studied in SCGY at the University of Science and Technology of China.
